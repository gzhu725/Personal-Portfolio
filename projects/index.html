AICS - Registration Portal

made with:

the acton institute of computer science (aics) is a nonprofit organization which provides free, accessible, computer science education to young students. i began working at aics as a python instructor and an operations member. as an operations member, the process of enrolling a student into a class was incredibly tedious.

this is where the registration portal comes to play. several student developers and i created this registration portal to faciliate the registration process without having operation members do the tedious work the registration portal can handle. additionally, the portal handles gradework, such that aics instructors can input assignment names and grades in a data table on the site. feel free to take a look!
